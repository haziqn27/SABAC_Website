Program Objective


The BBA (Hons) program is aimed at producing managers possessing analytical and creative abilities. The program encompasses sufficient knowledge of Mathematics, Economics, Accounting, Finance and Marketing. Due Consideration is given to communication skills, and seminars with a view to enable the graduates to express themselves confidently. Internships and field work provide the directly needed exposure to the real-world problems and practical approaches to their solutions. The Objective is to help the students in developing conceptual understanding thereby enabling them to apply their knowledge to solve the real world problems. Proficiency in computer usage is also one of the objectives. It is also aimed at providing students with a firm basis for undertaking higher studies in Management Sciences. The BBA (Hons) graduates can complete their MBA in just one and half years.
